How to Convert from Fahrenheit (°F) to Celsius (°C)

Temperature S in degrees Celsius (°C) is equal to temperature S in degrees Fahrenheit (°F) minus 32 times 5/9.

S(°C) = (S(°F) - 32) × 5/9
